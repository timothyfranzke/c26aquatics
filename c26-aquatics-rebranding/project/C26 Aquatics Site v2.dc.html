Our method

How we teach swimming

Every swimmer starts with water comfort and safety, then builds stroke technique in a structured progression. Because classes never exceed four swimmers, coaches adjust to each child or adult in real time — so skills lock in faster and progress is visible from the very first lesson.

Swimmers advance through clear levels, and coaches share written progress notes so you always know what comes next.

About C26

Our story

Our mission

Build confident, capable swimmers — and keep them in the water for life.

How C26 started

One pool, one standard

We started C26 because we were tired of crowded rec programs and a new instructor every session. Swimming is a life skill and a lifelong sport — it deserves better than a rotating cast of teenagers and a borrowed lane.

So we built a single home for swimming in Kansas City: one indoor pool, one career coaching staff, and one standard that follows a swimmer from their first lesson to their first triathlon — or their first podium.

Today that means small classes, real strength & conditioning, and coaches who know every swimmer by name.
