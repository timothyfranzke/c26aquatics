How to use these

Pick one signature seam. The lane-line (04) or hatch band (05) is the most ownable — use it only where light meets dark, maybe 2–3 times per page.

Add one quiet texture. Caustics (07) or the watermark (10) make the navy fields feel like water without competing with copy.

Tinted fields (06) are the lowest-risk upgrade — pure color rhythm, no new shapes, fully on-brand.

Don't stack them. A diagonal cut + caustics + watermark on one seam is the "too busy" you want to avoid. One divider plus one texture per section is plenty.
