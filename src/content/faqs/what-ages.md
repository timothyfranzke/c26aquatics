We work with swimmers from **6 months through adulthood**.

- **Lessons:** Parent & Tot starting at 6 months, Child Lessons (4-7), Youth Lessons (8-12), Teen & Adult Lessons (13+), and private lessons at any age.
- **Competitive team:** Bronze (ages 7-10) through Platinum senior elite (ages 14-18).

If you're not sure where your swimmer fits, we'll evaluate them in the water and recommend the right group.
