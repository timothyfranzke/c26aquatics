Our group lesson classes are capped at **four swimmers per coach** — always. That's not a marketing number; it's how the curriculum is built. With four kids in the water, every swimmer gets real instruction and real reps every session, and coaches can adjust to each child in real time. We also offer **semi-private** (2 swimmers) and **private** (1-on-1) sessions for families who want even more individual attention.
