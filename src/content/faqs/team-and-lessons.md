Yes — both. C26 is a single program with two on-ramps: **swim lessons** for kids and adults learning the basics or refining strokes, and a **year-round competitive team** for swimmers ready to train and race. The coaching philosophy and staff are the same across both — so kids who start in lessons have a clear, supported path onto the team when they're ready.
