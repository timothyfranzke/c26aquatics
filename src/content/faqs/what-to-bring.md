Keep it simple:

- A well-fitting swimsuit (no swim diapers needed over age 3 unless your coach asks)
- **Swim diapers** for infants and toddlers — both reusable and disposable are fine
- Goggles (we recommend, not required for the first lesson)
- A towel and a dry change of clothes
- A water bottle

Long hair should be tied back or in a swim cap. We have caps and a small loaner-goggles bin at the pool if you forget.
