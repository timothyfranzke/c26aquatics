Teen & Adult Lessons are for anyone who wants to learn to swim, get back in the water after time away, or clean up strokes for triathlon, fitness, or just feeling at home in a lap pool. Zero judgement. Real progress.

**What we work on**

- Water comfort and breathing — the foundation everything else is built on
- Freestyle and backstroke technique appropriate to your goals
- Endurance development — building from 25s to continuous laps
- Optional: breaststroke, sidestroke, and stroke refinement for fitness swimmers

**Format**

Small group classes of up to four swimmers, 45 minutes, scheduled around evenings and weekends to fit working adults. Private sessions also available if you'd prefer one-on-one — see our Private Lessons track.
