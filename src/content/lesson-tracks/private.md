Private Lessons are one swimmer with one coach for the full session. The fastest progression we offer — and the right fit when you need to work around an unusual schedule, address a specific skill gap, or move quickly toward a goal.

**Who it's for**

- Kids whose schedules don't line up with our group class times
- Swimmers preparing for summer-league tryouts on a tight timeline
- Adults learning to swim who want privacy and personalized attention
- Triathletes and fitness swimmers refining freestyle technique

**Format**

30- or 45-minute sessions, scheduled directly with the coach. Sessions can be booked as one-offs or as a package. All C26 lesson coaches are USA Swimming registered and SafeSport certified.

**Note:** Private sessions book up fast in summer. Reach out early to lock in your preferred coach and time.
