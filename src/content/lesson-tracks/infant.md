Parent & Tot is C26's gentlest on-ramp to swimming. A caregiver gets in the water with your little one, and our coach guides the class through songs, holds, submersions at your child's pace, and the safety basics every Kansas City family needs.

**What we work on**

- Water comfort and breath control
- Safe entries and exits (back float, wall climb)
- Submersions, blowing bubbles, and floats
- Parent confidence — how to support your child in the water at home and on vacation

**Format**

Up to six parent-child pairs per class, 30 minutes, in our warm indoor teaching pool. Weekly or twice-weekly sessions available.
