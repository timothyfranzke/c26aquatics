Youth Lessons are for school-age kids who want to swim well — whether that's keeping up at the pool with friends, getting ready for summer-league tryouts, or building toward year-round competitive swimming at C26.

**What we work on**

- Legal freestyle and backstroke technique
- Introduction to breaststroke and butterfly
- Endurance — building up to swimming 100 yards continuously
- Diving from the side and basic competitive turns

**Format**

Up to four swimmers per class, 45 minutes, once or twice weekly. Coaches share written progress notes so families always know what's next — and what it takes to move into Bronze.
