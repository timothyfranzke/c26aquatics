Child Lessons are where most C26 swimmers start their independent swimming journey. Classes are kept to four swimmers max with a single coach, so kids get real reps and real feedback every session.

**What we work on**

- Independent floats, glides, and treading water
- Front- and back-crawl fundamentals — body line, kick, side breathing
- Safe entries, wall touches, and recovery to the side
- Confidence in deeper water as skills progress

**Format**

Up to four swimmers per class, 30 minutes, twice weekly or once weekly options. Levels are reviewed every six weeks with a written progress note for parents.

Kids who graduate Child Lessons are evaluated for our Youth track or — if they're ready — our Bronze pre-competitive group.
