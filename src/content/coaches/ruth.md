Ruth Calloway leads our Bronze and Silver training groups and oversees the C26 swim lesson program from infant through teen. A former collegiate swimmer, Ruth has been coaching in the Kansas City metro for over a decade and is the coach most parents meet first — the one who turns a hesitant five-year-old into a confident freestyler.

Ruth's strength is the bridge: getting kids comfortable enough in the water that they want to keep coming back, then teaching the technical fundamentals — body line, breath control, kick — that hold up when they move into the competitive groups. Every C26 lesson coach is trained on Ruth's progression and reports to her each week.

Ruth is ASCA-certified, a USA Swimming member coach, and SafeSport current.
