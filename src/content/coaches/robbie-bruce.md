Robbie Bruce is an athlete-centered coach built on one belief: better technique creates better swimmers. Known for a detailed eye and a hands-on style, he uses underwater and above-water video analysis and individualized feedback to improve efficiency, body position, power, timing, and feel for the water. He works with competitive age-groupers, collegiate swimmers, and out-of-state athletes who travel to Kansas City to train with him.

Robbie is also nationally recognized in open water swimming — one of the Midwest's leading open water coaches. He has guided athletes to top-five finishes at Open Water Zones, national-level collegiate 5K championships, and the professional ranks of triathlon, and has coached swimmers through everything from their first open water race to the English Channel. He works with the High School Prep and Pre-Senior groups and leads the C26 open water program.
