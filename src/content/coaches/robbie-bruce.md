Robbie Bruce founded C26 Aquatics on a simple idea: every swimmer in Kansas City deserves career-coach attention, warm water, and a clear path forward. With more than 25 years on deck — from learn-to-swim through national-level age group athletes — Robbie has built the C26 program around two non-negotiables: small training pods and measurable progress.

Before launching C26, Robbie coached age group and senior swimmers across the Midwest, sent athletes to YMCA Nationals, NCSA Junior Nationals, and USA Swimming Sectionals, and worked with collegiate programs as a stroke consultant. He holds ASCA Level 4 certification, is a registered USA Swimming coach in good standing, and is current on U.S. Center for SafeSport training.

When he's not pacing the deck at our Overland Park pool, Robbie is usually breaking down underwater video, building the next training cycle, or talking shop with parents about the long game of swimmer development.
