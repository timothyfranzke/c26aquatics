Silver is where age group swimming gets serious. Practices run three times a week for 90 minutes, and our swimmers train with intent — every lap has a purpose, and every kid leaves practice knowing what they worked on.

**What we focus on**

- Stroke refinement and underwater work
- Aerobic base development with appropriate yardage
- Race strategy across 50s, 100s, and 200s
- IM training as a foundation for long-term swimmer development
- Goal-setting and stroke-by-stroke video review

**Meet expectations**

Silver swimmers attend a regular USA Swimming meet schedule — typically one meet per month during the season, with the goal of qualifying for our spring championship meet. Attendance at the championship meet is the team-wide goal for every Silver swimmer by end of season.

**Time commitment**

We ask Silver swimmers to attend at least two of three weekly practices. Consistency is what makes the training work.
