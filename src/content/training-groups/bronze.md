Bronze is the entry point to competitive swimming at C26 — built for kids who already love the water and are ready to learn what training looks like. We meet twice a week for an hour, with practices that balance technique work on all four strokes, intro starts and turns, and the kind of structured set work that turns lesson skills into race-ready skills.

**What we focus on**

- Legal freestyle, backstroke, breaststroke, and butterfly
- Streamline body line off every wall
- Basic interval training and pace awareness
- Meet readiness — how to warm up, read a heat sheet, and race

**Meet expectations**

Bronze swimmers are encouraged but not required to attend our intra-squad meet and one or two local USA Swimming developmental meets per season. The focus is fun and exposure — not times.

**How to join**

New families start with a free water evaluation with Coach Ruth. We'll place each swimmer in the group that fits today and outline what's next.
