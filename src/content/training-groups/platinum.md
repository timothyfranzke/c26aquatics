Platinum is our senior elite group — built for high school athletes training at a national qualifying level and preparing for collegiate swimming. Nine sessions a week, including two morning practices and a long-course Saturday, with full dryland and recovery programming.

**What we focus on**

- High-yardage aerobic base with race-pace integration
- Event specialization and championship-season tapering
- College recruiting support — video, time-progression sheets, coach communication
- Strength and conditioning twice a week with a credentialed S&C coach
- Race-day routine, mindset, and recovery protocols

**Meet expectations**

Platinum swimmers compete on the championship circuit: Sectionals, Speedo Sectionals, Futures, and Junior Nationals depending on cuts. We build the calendar around peak meets and protect training blocks between them.

**Time commitment**

Platinum is a daily commitment. The expectation is that swimming is the priority sport, and the training group is the priority team. We work closely with each family on academics, recruiting timelines, and recovery.
