Gold is our championship-track group for serious age group swimmers chasing senior cuts. Five practices a week plus an optional Saturday long-course session put the work in — and our coach-to-swimmer ratio means nothing gets missed.

**What we focus on**

- Aerobic capacity and threshold training
- Race-specific pace work and lactate sets
- Advanced underwater dolphin kick and breakout work
- Dryland strength and mobility (two sessions per week)
- Championship preparation across short course and long course seasons

**Meet expectations**

Gold swimmers are expected to attend the full team meet calendar, including the spring championship meet and at least one long-course meet in summer. Many Gold swimmers qualify for state championship meets, Sectionals, and Speedo Sectionals depending on times.

**Time commitment**

Five practices per week with very limited absences. We work with families on school sports, music, and academic commitments — but Gold is built on consistency.
